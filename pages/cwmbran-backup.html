cwmbran 2: bramble boogaloo

- 13th of November, 2024 -



I'm back on the hill again, having scaled the treacherously slimy staircase to the decking, with a coffee and my mate Csopi. There are a few doves around, spread out and enjoying their space, in the canopy of a true giant of a tree that may as well be half a forest.



my mate Csopi


I've been living in the Big Smoke for about half a year, and I really missed hanging out with neighbourhood cats, there weren't nearly as many around. All kinds of memories started coming back from where I left off, like refilling the bird (squirrel) feeder every morning from a bag of peanuts the size of a small fridge, checking the window every time I dropped by the kitchen for something to see if Csopi or one of the others showed up (Csopi was a vagrant back then, but owing to various territorial and power dynamic shifts has become a regular), etc.


Where do you end a blog post? If you promise yourself that you'll write another one soon, it becomes a less daunting q2uestion. On that note, thank you for reading, and see you soon.
